About Us

Jai Hind! National Veterans Digital Federation ek swatantra manch hai jiska mukhya uddeshya desh bhar ke military veterans ko ek digital sutra mein pirona hai.

Hamara Mission

Hum chahte hain ki village level se lekar national level tak har veteran ki awaaz ek ho[cite: 1]. Hum veterans ko unki pension (SPARSH), medical (ECHS), aur anya sarkari yojnaon ki sahi jankari pradan karte hain[cite: 1].

Hum Kya Karte Hain?
  • Ek centralized portal jahan har veteran aur sangthan register kar sake[cite: 1].
  • Grievance escalation system taaki samasyaon ka samadhan sahi level par ho sake[cite: 1].
  • Latest circulars aur welfare news ki sahi jankari[cite: 1].
