Proud Indian Army Veteran Initiative

Introduction

Main swayam ek Indian Army Veteran hu aur filhal RO Gandhinagar Region (Gujarat) mein apni sevaayein de raha hu. Ek veteran hone ke naate, main un sabhi digital samasyaon ko samajhta hu jo hamare retirees ko SPARSH migration, PPO issues aur welfare schemes ke dauran aati hain.

Our Goal

ESM HELP ka uddeshya hamare Ex-Servicemen, Veer Naris aur Dependents ko ek hi platform par authentic circulars, jobs aur pension guides pradan karna hai. Hamara focus transparency aur sateek jankari par hai taaki kisi bhi veteran bhai ko bhatakna na pade.
