Humara Goal (Our Vision)

Swayam ek foji hone ke naate, main un sabhi digital aur administrative chunaotiyon ko nikat se samajhta hu jo hamare retirees ko legacy bank se **SPARSH Portal** par migrate hone ke baad ya welfare schemes apply karte waqt aati hain.

**ESM HELP** portal ka ekmatra lakshya hamare Ex-Servicemen, Veer Naris aur Dependents ko sateek circulars, defense quota jobs, ECHS medical card updates aur CSD processes se judi 100% authentic jankari pradan karna hai taaki kisi bhi jawan ko bhatakna na pade.
