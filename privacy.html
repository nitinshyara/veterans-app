Privacy Policy

Transparency & Safety for our Online Community

Last Updated: 17 May 2026

ESM HELP (`esmhelp.com`) par hamare visitors ki privacy hamare liye sabse upar hai. Hum standard AdSense policies aur user data security niyam ka kadai se palan karte hain.

1. Information Collection & Use

Hum kisi bhi veteran ki vyaktigat ya gopniya pension bank details, passwords ya identity proofs yahan collect ya store nahi karte hain. Hum ek swatantra jankari pradan karne wala portal hain.

2. Log Files

Anya sabhi professional websites ki tarah, ESM HELP bhi log files ka upayog karti hai. In files ke andar keval internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, aur exit pages ki sankhya shamil hoti hai jo kisi bhi vyaktigat pehchan se judi nahi hoti.

3. Cookies & Google AdSense

Google hamari website par vigyapan (Ads) dikhane ke liye ek third-party vendor ke roop mein cookies ka upayog karta hai. Google dwara DART cookie ka upayog hamare users ko unke browsing interest ke aadhar par relevant ads dikhane ki anumati deta hai. Users chahein toh Google ad network settings mein ja kar cookies ko disable kar sakte hain.

4. Third-Party Links

Hamari website par sarkari portals (Jaise SPARSH, PCDA, ECHS) ke links diye gaye hain. Un external websites par jaane ke baad unki apni privacy policy laagu hoti hai, isliye hum unke content ki koi legal zimmedari nahi lete.
